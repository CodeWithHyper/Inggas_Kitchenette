About Us

Ingga’s Kitchenette is a premier catering service provider dedicated to bringing people together through the love of food. Founded with a passion for authentic cuisine, we specialize in creating memorable dining experiences for weddings, corporate events, and family gatherings.

Our team takes pride in sourcing fresh, local ingredients to craft dishes that not only taste amazing but also warm the heart. Whether you need food packs for a meeting or a full buffet setup for your big day, we are here to serve you with excellence.
